  • B.A.R.D ROBOTS (files for each robot type & specifications)
    Gameplay guides recognise two robot categories: Machine‑Gun Type and Flamethrower Type. Weak‑points include optics/sensors, weapons & fuel tanks, and the Atomic Battery; overheating ejects the battery for a non‑explosive disable. [1](https://gamerant.com/atomfall-how-deal-with-fight-destroy-bard-robots/)[2](blob:https://outlook.office.com/d8aa9298-550b-4053-8e47-953c3a9b9c2c)
    Class B Robot Unit 45 — (Machine‑Gun Type)

    Role: patrol & door security; stabilised belt‑fed fire.

    Field notes: Prioritise the optics (“eyes”). Sustained hits force overheat → battery eject. Avoid long, open sightlines.

    Source: enemy/robot guides. [1](https://gamerant.com/atomfall-how-deal-with-fight-destroy-bard-robots/)

    Orange B.A.R.D Robot — (Machine‑Gun Type)

    Notes: Hazard‑panel livery frequently seen around Interchange & exterior compounds; same gun platform behaviour as other MG units.

    Context: B.A.R.D location lists & community documentation. [3](blob:https://outlook.office.com/976515e3-36c7-4749-975c-025c401ddfdf)

    White B.A.R.D Robot — (Machine‑Gun Type)

    Notes: Common on lab/interior patrols; standard MG behaviour.

    Context: community documentation. [3](blob:https://outlook.office.com/976515e3-36c7-4749-975c-025c401ddfdf)

    Black B.A.R.D Robot — (Machine‑Gun Type)

    Notes: Low‑visibility finish for yards/night posts; treat as high‑threat at range.

    Context: community documentation. [3](blob:https://outlook.office.com/976515e3-36c7-4749-975c-025c401ddfdf)

    Flamethrower Variant — (Flamethrower Type)

    Notes: Shielded sensors; visible red fuel tanks are weak points. Use distance and cover; never commit in tight corridors.

    Combat tips & weak‑points: flamethrower write‑ups. [2](blob:https://outlook.office.com/d8aa9298-550b-4053-8e47-953c3a9b9c2c)

    Heavy Machine‑Gun Variant — (Machine‑Gun Type)

    Notes: Long‑range suppression; smoke/hard cover; do not repeat‑peek. Blinding optics triggers overheat → battery eject for disable.

    Disable strategy & categories. [1](https://gamerant.com/atomfall-how-deal-with-fight-destroy-bard-robots/)

    Tip: If you’re publishing robot images per variant, keep filenames consistent (e.g., /assets/robots/bard_robot_flamethrower.png) and they’ll drop into existing pages.
